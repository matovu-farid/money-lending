**Test:** On an active loan detail page, enter a payment amount in the simulator and click "Simulate".
**Expected:** Two side-by-side cards appear showing Current vs After Simulated Payment with correct figures; edge case messages appear correctly (full payoff, partial interest only).
**Why human:** Client-side calculation display and visual layout require a running app.

**Test:** After cron has generated notifications, open the app and inspect the bell icon in the top bar.
**Expected:** Bell shows a badge with unread count; clicking opens dropdown listing notifications; "Mark all as read" clears the badge.
**Why human:** Requires a running cron job and database state to test realistically.

### 4. Watchlist threshold accuracy

**Test:** Identify a loan where daysOverdue is near 30 (e.g., 29 vs 31 days).
**Expected:** Loan at 29 days does NOT appear on watchlist; loan at 30+ days DOES appear.
**Why human:** Requires real loan data with known overdue values to confirm boundary condition.

### 5. Blacklist safeguard at loan issuance

**Test:** Attempt to issue a new loan to a customer with "Blacklisted" status.
**Expected:** A clear validation error appears blocking the loan creation; no partial record is created.
**Why human:** Requires UI interaction through the loan wizard with a blacklisted customer.
